class page for a 2006 Columbia anthropology class on ["Thing Theory"](https://www.columbia.edu/~sf2220/Thing/web-content/Pages/Opening.html). contains student-written "object ethnographies" on various objects, like the Toyota Prius, blogs, and trash. cool stuff, a bit of a time capsule. there are [two](https://www.columbia.edu/~sf2220/Thing/web-content/Pages/balikbayanbox.html) [pieces](https://www.columbia.edu/~sf2220/Thing/web-content/Pages/johanna2.html) about balikbayan boxes, definitely an anthropologically rich part of filipino immigrant culture.
