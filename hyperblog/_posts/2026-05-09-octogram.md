[octogram by jack lance](https://jacklance.github.io/octogram): fun puzzle game about guessing at the meanings of eight-letter strings

<details>

<summary>more info</summary>

this was a fun one to show to friends and work on together. i still remember turning one of these over and over in my mind while invigilating an exam (i think it was BLEDRITE). when i still had multiple rows left, matthew told me he managed to get every level while streaming with friends. looking at it again, exactly one level still eludes me. i also found out that jack lance died recently, and tragically young. RIP. it's nice that his legacy, in the form of fun puzzles and such, gets to live on.
